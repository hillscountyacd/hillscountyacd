ADA 33: Celebrate. Learn. Share: Advancing Access Through Innovative Technology

Save the Date!

Join us for refreshments, vendors, an award ceremony, and lots of fun!

Created by:

WHO: Citizens who work, play, and or live in the Hillsborough County area interested in ensuring opportunities for the full inclusion and participation of citizens with disabilities throughout the community.

WHAT: A Technology Advances for Persons with Disabilities Resource Expo and Hillsborough County Advocacy Awards Ceremony for citizens with disabilities and the public to generate awareness, share information, and demonstrate equipment or technology to enhance independent living and quality of life for all. Past Alliance events have attracted approximately 150-200 people.

WHEN: July 26, 2023, 10 AM − 2 PM

WHERE: Lesley “Les” Miller Jr. All People’s Community Park & Life Center

6105 E Sligh Ave, Tampa, FL 33617

ABOUT THE LOCATION: Hillsborough County’s largest adaptive playground. The 10,000-square-foot, ADA-compliant playground has an adventure theme and includes numerous play units designed to encourage children to stay active and have fun. In addition to traditional elements like swings and slides, the playground features rope- and rockclimbing structures, musical elements, and synthetic turf.
